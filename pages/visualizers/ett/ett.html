A forest, folded into a sequence. A sequence, folded into a BST.

An Euler Tour Tree stores each tree in a forest as its Euler tour — every edge visited twice, a genuine 2n−1‑length sequence — kept inside a balanced BST. link and cut become substring splice/extract operations; connected(u, v) becomes "same BST." It's easy to confuse with Link‑Cut Trees, but it solves undirected forest connectivity with completely different machinery.
